Every Piece Matters for Churchill

  • BySharon Squires- Hansen|
  • 23 Comments|
  • MAR-15-2016

Churchill Consulting is pleased to announce that it has pledged to help a Cambodian family relocate from slums in 2016 as part of Raw Impact’s fundraising initiative ‘Every Piece Matters’.

The project, which aims to help 100 of the poorest families in Cambodia, involves raising $5,000AUD that will be put toward the provision of land and housing for these families. Individual families can be selected and ‘pledged to’ as part of this initiative.

Director of Churchill Consulting, David Prendiville, believes it will be a rewarding and beneficial experience for all involved. “The families living in these slums are in desperate need of assistance, and we are in the fortunate position of being able to help them. We also hope to involve our team members in the process of fundraising as part of our ‘Giving Back’ program.”

Team members from the management consultancy will underake a range of activities over the next few months to fundraise for the cause.

Every Piece Matters was created by Raw Impact off the back of an announcement by the Cambodian government that a bridge would be built between Phnom Penh and Gunty’s Island. Unfortunately, this meant that many families living in slums around these areas would need to relocate; without access to safe housing, education, and jobs.

For more information about the inspiring work being done by Raw Impact and how you can get involved in this initiative visit www.rawimpact.org.

For more information about Churchill Consulting, please visit the website: churchill.com.au.
